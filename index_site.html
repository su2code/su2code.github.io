What is SU2?

The SU2 suite is an open-source collection of C++ based software tools for performing Partial Differential Equation (PDE) analysis and solving PDE-constrained optimization problems. The toolset was designed with Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) and aerodynamic shape optimization in mind, but it has been (and keeps being) extended to treat arbitrary sets of governing equations such as potential flow, elasticity, electrodynamics, chemically-reacting flows, and many others.

Free and open. Always.

SU2 is released under the open-source license LGPL 2.1, and it's under active development by the members of the SU2 community.
